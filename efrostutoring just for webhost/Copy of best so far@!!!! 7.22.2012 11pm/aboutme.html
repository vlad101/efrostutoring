ABOUT ME

I have more than 6 years of intensive research in chemistry and I have started my own tutoring business in order to help students who really need help in Math and Science; that is what I have been doing full-time since 2005. My first tutoring experience was as a self-employed tutor in 2005 and I have been teaching and tutoring for most of my free time since then. I am mentoring students at all levels of science and mathematics on a regular basis. I am currently an active member of the American Chemical Society. As a graduate student I work as a teaching assistant in charge of chemistry labs, grading exams, keeping records of grades, laboratory safety inspections, and I aid in other relevant tasks.
Many of my students score above average on their exams. I try to simplify the material wherever possible and design my instruction to each individual's needs. I am a strong believer in the importance of education and emphasize that education is the only path to success
